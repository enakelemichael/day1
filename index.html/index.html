Lagos City at Night

The city of Lagos is often regarded as the economic capital of Nigeria. It is a small city and is highly overpopulated.

lagos-night

However, a completely different scenario is painted at night time when the day has come to an end and the busy people running around are heading home. Lagos becomes beautiful and shines bright like a diamond painting another completely different picture.Here are a few pictures to prove to you that Lagos is a very beautiful city and comes alive at night!
